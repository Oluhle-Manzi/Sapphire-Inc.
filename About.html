About Us

The purpose of the entire CTU-Buddy project is to create an educational system that revolutionizes the learning experience for students at City Technical University (CTU). The project aims to provide a comprehensive and interactive platform, CTU-Buddy, that enhances the academic journey of CTU students in numerous ways.


Vision

CTU-Buddy is a website created for students who attend Suncrest College to help them with their studies. Students will be able to access their timetable, discuss any issues with their curriculum and share materials to help other students.
